- **Global Hair Extension Market:** ~$5.88 billion by 2030 (7.5% CAGR)

- **Key Drivers:** Demand for premium hair, personalization, social media influence

### Target Customer Profile
- Licensed hair stylists with active client base
- Salon owners looking for reliable supply chains
- Independent texture specialists (especially in curly/coily hair niche)

### Competitive Advantage
- ✅ Existing network of stylists (organic customer base)
- ✅ Direct access to end users = feedback loop
- ✅ Lower overhead than retail-first brands

## Marketing Strategy

### Phase 1: Launch (Months 1-3)
- Leverage existing stylist network
- In-person appointments with product samples
- Social media (Instagram is key for hair industry)
- Word of mouth referrals

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-9)
- Referral program for stylists (discount on next order)
- Attend hair industry trade shows
- Collaborate with influencers (stylists with large followings)
- Google Business listing

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 10+)
- Expanded product line
- Possibly: own hair processing/ coloring in-house
- Build brand recognition

### Key Channels
- **Instagram** - Primary platform (hair pics, transformations)
- **TikTok** - Tutorial clips, before/afters
- **Referrals** - Word of mouth is gold in this industry
- **Local hair shows** - Network directly
