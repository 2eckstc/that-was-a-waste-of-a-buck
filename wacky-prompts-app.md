## APP CONCEPT
A social app where users share wacky, weird, and outrageous AI prompts they've tried, along with the hilarious results. Think "Fail Blog" but for AI chatbot outputs.

---

## CORE FEATURES

### 1. Feed (Home)
- Scrollable feed of top/wacky prompts
- Each post shows: Prompt + AI Response + Platform tag (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.)
- Upvote/like system
- Share button

### 2. Categories
- 🤪 **Hilarious** - Funny prompts that went wrong
- 😱 **Outrageous** - Unexpected wild responses  
- 🖤 **Dark Mode** - Edgy/dark humor prompts
- 🔮 **Weird** - Just plain bizarre
- 💕 **Wholesome** - Surprisingly sweet AI moments
- 🤖 **AI Art** - Midjourney/DALL-E wild outputs
- 🎮 **Gaming** - Prompts for game characters

### 3. Submit a Post
- User enters their prompt
- Selects AI tool used
- Adds the "outcome" (screenshot or text)
- Optional: Add context/what they were going for

### 4. Leaderboard
- Top prompts of the week
- Most viral
- Best "wacky" category

### 5. Search
- Search by keyword, AI tool, or category

### Content Sourcing Ideas

**Scraping Social Media (X/Twitter, Instagram, YouTube):**
⚠️ WARNING: Scraping major platforms violates their Terms of Service. Can get you banned or in legal trouble.

**Better Legal Alternatives:**
1. **User Submissions** - Build a form where users submit their best wacky prompts + AI responses
2. **Moderation Queue** - Review submissions before publishing (prevents issues)
3. **API Access** - Twitter/X has an API (costs money but legal)
4. **Creative Commons** - Search for CC-licensed AI fails/mistakes
5. **"Submit Your Fail"** - Create a landing page encouraging people to share

**The Smart Model:**
- Users submit → You moderate → Publish to feed
- All content is user-generated (legal!)
- Builds community engagement
